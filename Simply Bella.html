One of my favorite things to make are tassel earrings, When you first start making them it is kind of frustrating, but once you get the hang of it making them, they are a blast to make!

What you will need to make these tassle earings are:

Sewing Thread

Jump rings

Fish Wire Ear Hooks

Scizzors

A Wide Mouth Cup

Here is what you do: Start by wrapping the sewing thread around the wide mouthed cup until there is a thick wrapping of thread around the cup.

Then slid the thread off of the cup, and add two jump rings(one on each side).

Now it is time to make the tie off (so the jump rings don’t slide around) take a long piece of thread and tie off right under the 1st jump ring tie it once and begin wrapping the thread around, you need to wrap it around about 20 times. Now, tie it off and repeat on the other side.

After you have tied off both sides it is time to cut them apart. Take your soon to be earrings and cut them in half.

Now you have to determine the length you want them and cut them to that size.

Once you have done that just add your ear wire hooks and you are done. You have just made an amazing pair of earring! Go you!
